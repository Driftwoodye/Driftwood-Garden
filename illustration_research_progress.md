## Notes and exact-name search history

- Cedrus atlantica = Cedrus libani var./subsp. atlantica; also searched Pinus atlantica and Abies atlantica.
- Cleistocactus strausii = Pilocereus strausii, Borzicactus strausii, Cephalocereus straussii, Cereus strausii, Denmoza strausii.
- Brunnera macrophylla historical basionym searches use Myosotis macrophylla / Anchusa myosotidiflora; unrelated Anchusa macrophylla is rejected.
- Veronica salicifolia = Hebe salicifolia, Panoxis salicifolia.
- Oenothera fruticosa searches also used Oenothera linearis, Oenothera incana, Onagra linkiana and Sphaerostigma-related names; no exact reusable plate was verified.
- Staphisagria requienii was also checked as Delphinium requienii and Delphinium pictum subsp. requienii. Results located were photographs or taxonomic pages, not a clearly reusable exact botanical plate.
- Gloriosa modesta was also checked under the former genus Littonia. No exact reusable historical plate was verified.
- Nandina domestica searches returned modern photographs and general taxon pages; no exact reusable botanical/scientific illustration with clear rights was verified.
- Gunnera killipiana searches did not produce a clearly reusable exact botanical/scientific illustration.

## Acceptance rules

Accept only exact taxa, cultivars, varieties or colour forms with clear legal reuse rights. Reject ordinary photographs, related-species substitutions, wrong colour forms and modern artwork with unclear rights.
